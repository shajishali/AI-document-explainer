SAMPLE LEGAL AGREEMENT

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on this date between Party A ("Provider") and Party B ("Client").

1. SERVICES
The Provider agrees to provide legal document analysis services to the Client. These services include document review, risk assessment, and plain-language explanations.

2. TERM
This Agreement shall commence on the date of execution and continue for a period of one (1) year, unless terminated earlier as provided herein.

3. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days written notice to the other party. In the event of breach of any material term, the non-breaching party may terminate immediately.

4. LIABILITY
The Provider's liability under this Agreement shall be limited to the amount paid by the Client for services rendered. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all information shared during the course of this Agreement. This obligation shall survive termination of the Agreement.

6. INDEMNIFICATION
Each party agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the other party from any claims arising out of their respective performance under this Agreement.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction where the Provider is located.

8. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings.
